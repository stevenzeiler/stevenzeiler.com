Zeilermark Currency

What are zeilermarks?

Zeilermark is a currency that represents on hour of Steven Zeiler's time per unit, which exist and can be owned using the ripple protocol.

How does one obtain zeilermarks?

First trust ~stevenzeiler or r4EwBWxrx5HxYRyisfGzMto3AT8FZiYdWk on Ripple for the currency code ZMK to enable zeilermarks for your ripple account. Then purchase (ZMK) issued by ~stevenzeiler on the ripple exchange.

How does one use zeilermarks?

To trade one zeilermark for one hour of Steven Zeiler's time, send a message to Steven Zeiler indicating how many hours you want to redeem, the task you would like performed, and an expected timeline. After approval by Steven send the desired number of (ZMK) to ~stevenzeiler. Note the right is reserved to delay all requests by up to two weeks from request date, and to limit total (ZMK) redeemed to ten per week.
